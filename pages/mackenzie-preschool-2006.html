Mackenzie 2006 Pre-School Mackenzie's teacher's name is Miss Sandy. She was a perfect match for Mackenzie because she's a very hands-on teacher. She let the kids get their hands dirty! Mackenzie remembers when Miss Sandy made a pile of chalk dust out on the pavement for the kids to put their hands in to make hand prints on the pavement. When they were learning about the 5 senses, Miss Sandy gave them each a plateful of chocolate pudding to "touch" and squish around. Then they got to eat it! Boy, Miss Sandy is brave! Miss Sandy also has two gerbils in her classroom - Snowball and Sparkle. Mackenzie - along with every other kid in her class - loved when a toilet paper or paper towel roll emptied so she can bring the tube in for the gerbils to nibble on.
