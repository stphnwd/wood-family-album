Mom's Art

PaintShop Pro, Virtual University, and a lifetime of creativity

♥ ♥ ♥

Mom taught herself PaintShop Pro through online courses at Virtual University and never looked back. Starting with a beginner's Web Page Design class — where she wrote "I am very excited to be a part of Virtual University! I hope to create my first web page using what I learn in class" — she went on to master digital art, complete five-week intensive courses, earn a score of 21 out of 25 on her final exam, and eventually write her own tutorial to teach others.

What started as a hobby became a passion. She created signature graphics for her online friends, experimented with brushes, vectors, and photo manipulation, and built this very website to share it all. These pages are a tribute to her creativity, her dedication, and the joy she found in learning something new.
