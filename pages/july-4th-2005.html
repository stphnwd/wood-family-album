July 4th, 2005 — Smith's Point Park

♥ ♥ ♥

Megan and Ryan at Smith's Point Park July 4th 2005 Smith's Point Park , Fire Island New York and Family BBQ A wonderful day was spent at Tricia and Chris's home. Weather was great and pool was 'warm' thanks to heated pool! Yes, all us women loved it. Before heading to the BBQ, I went to the beach with daughter Diane. her hubby Michael, and their children Ryan (12) and Megan (10). We went to Smith's Point on Fire Island which is about 10 miles south of where I live. We spent about 5 hours there. The kids had a great time playing in the ocean. Megan went shell collecting Michael, Ryan and Megan Testing the waters!
