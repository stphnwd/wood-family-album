Jack 2006 Pre-School Jack's teacher's name is Miss Danielle. She was a perfect match for Jack! She's very fun-loving and silly and since Jack is usually a clown, they were made for each other. Jack liked all the silly names she made up for the kids in his class (although Jack said he didn't have one!). Jack's favorite part of the day was always either gym or going outside. But really, his favorite part was anything that he did with his friend J.J. so we credit Miss Danielle for Jack and J.J. meeting each other! They would hug each other about 1 million times at the end of the day.
