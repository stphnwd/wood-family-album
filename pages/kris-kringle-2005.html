Kris Kringle Day 2005 Each year our family gets together for "Kris Kringle Day". We have been doing this for a number of years and each year we have a different "Host". This year my daughter Diane and her husband Michael invited us all to their home. We start at noon and end 'whenever'. We eat all day and open presents! We all had a very "special" gift given to us this year. My daughter Teresa and her new hubby Kelly flew in from Texas to surprise us all! And boy, was that ever a surprise. Tears were flowing for sure. This was the very first time that Teresa has been able to join us for our Kris Kringle Day. It made the day very special. We were missing a few people this year.
