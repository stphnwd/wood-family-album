Reunion 2001 Wood Family 20th Annual Reunion August 4, 2001 Pound Ridge NY A great time was had by all. Diane and Michael Kraus were our host and hostess this year. They did a magnificent job. We had baseball, 2 legged races, egg toss, water balloon toss, and tug of war. There were games for the kids as well as the adults. We also had tattoo's and face painting.
