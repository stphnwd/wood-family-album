Jack & Mackenzie

Twins — Born February 7, 2001 — Children of Tricia and Chris

♥ ♥ ♥

Jack and Mackenzie are the twin children of my daughter Tricia and her husband Chris. Born on February 7, 2001, these two have been a double blessing from day one. From their first Christmas in matching outfits, to being "Cool Dude and Dudette" at 18 months, to graduating pre-school and heading off to kindergarten — every milestone has been twice the fun. Jack and Mackenzie have graduated Pre-School! In September 2006 they started kindergarten.
